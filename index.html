# Privacy Policy for Family Loop **Last Updated:** December 24, 2024 ## Introduction Family Loop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information. ## Information We Collect ### Information You Provide - **Account Information:** Email address, display name, and profile information when you create an account - **Interaction Logs:** Records of communication you manually log with your contacts (dates, types, notes) - **Goals and Events:** Communication goals and family events you create in the app ### Information Automatically Collected (With Your Permission) - **Contacts:** Names and phone numbers from your device's contact list - **Call Logs:** Call history to automatically detect when you've called contacts (Android only) - **SMS Messages:** Text message metadata (date, contact) to automatically detect communication (Android only) ## How We Use Your Information We use your information to: - Filter and organize your contacts intelligently - Track your communication patterns with family and friends - Automatically detect phone calls and text messages (if you enable this feature) - Calculate relationship health scores and provide insights - Send reminders about communication goals - Sync your data across your devices (via Firebase) ## Data Storage and Security ### On-Device Processing - **Call logs and SMS data are processed entirely on your device** - We do NOT upload your call logs, SMS content, or raw contact data to our servers - Only anonymized interaction metadata (timestamps, contact IDs) is stored in the cloud ### Cloud Storage (Firebase) We store the following in Google Firebase: - Your account information (email, display name) - Interaction logs you create (date, type, optional notes) - Communication goals and family events - Relationship insights and scores All cloud data is: - Encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) - Encrypted at rest - Protected by Firebase Authentication - Only accessible to your account ## Permissions We Request ### Required Permissions - **Contacts (READ_CONTACTS):** To load and filter your contact list - **Internet:** To sync data with Firebase and enable authentication ### Optional Permissions (You Control) - **Call Log (READ_CALL_LOG):** To automatically detect phone calls - *Android only* - **SMS (READ_SMS):** To automatically detect text messages - *Android only* **Important:** You can use Family Loop without granting call log or SMS permissions. These are optional features for automatic tracking. You can always track interactions manually. ## Data Sharing We do NOT: - Sell your personal information to third parties - Share your contact information with advertisers - Use your data for purposes other than providing the Family Loop service - Access the content of your text messages or calls We may share data only in these situations: - **With your consent:** If you explicitly agree to share specific information - **Legal requirements:** If required by law, court order, or government request - **Service providers:** Firebase (Google) hosts our data - see [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) ## Your Rights and Choices You have the right to: - **Access your data:** View all data we've collected about you - **Export your data:** Download a copy of your information (available in app settings) - **Delete your data:** Permanently delete your account and all associated data - **Revoke permissions:** Turn off automatic detection at any time - **Opt-out of features:** Use the app without SMS/call log access ### How to Exercise Your Rights - **Export Data:** Go to Settings → Data Management → Export All Data - **Delete Data:** Go to Settings → Data Management → Delete All Data - **Delete Account:** Contact us at support@familyloop.app - **Revoke Permissions:** Go to your device Settings → Apps → Family Loop → Permissions ## Data Retention - **Active Accounts:** We retain your data as long as your account is active - **Deleted Accounts:** Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion - **Inactive Accounts:** Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted after email notification ## Children's Privacy Family Loop is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we've collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by: - Posting the new policy in the app - Sending an email notification (if we have your email) - Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy ## Third-Party Services Family Loop uses these third-party services: - **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, database, and cloud storage - [Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) - **Google Sign-In:** Optional authentication method - [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) ## Contact Us If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: - **In-App:** Settings → Help & Support → Contact Us ## California Privacy Rights (CCPA) If you're a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA): - Right to know what personal information we collect - Right to delete your personal information - Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data) - Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights ## European Privacy Rights (GDPR) If you're in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): - Right of access, rectification, erasure, and data portability - Right to restrict processing - Right to object to processing - Right to withdraw consent Our legal basis for processing: - **Consent:** For optional features like automatic detection - **Contract:** To provide the Family Loop service - **Legitimate interests:** To improve and secure our service --- **By using Family Loop, you agree to this Privacy Policy.** If you do not agree, please do not use the app.
